C · Dot Matrix (Ring Feedback)

Compact rows where feedback is shown as a colored ring on each dot itself. Green ring = exact, yellow = misplaced, no ring = no match. Very dense, no scroll needed.

Option A is closest to Wordle. B feels like a premium card game. C is the most unique — novel feedback encoding that no other Mastermind app uses.
